Who are you?

The Delta Confederation is a Dual Universe political and commercial organization created in 2018. The goal always has been to create a link between people, and between other organizations. With it's city, partnerships and confederate organizations, the Delta has everything to be an important actor of the economical and political growth of Dual Universe.

Who is in charge of the Confederation?

The Delta Confederation is lead by the Legates. These are the people who make the choices. Though they aren't the ones who apply them. This is the job of the Delta Citizens, who democratically vote for each directive. Citizens can also propose ideas to the Delta Group if they get enough support. Are called Citizens :

  • Any direct Legate or Member of the Delta Confederation
  • Any Legate or Member of Confederate Organizations
  • Any other certified people with the Delta Citizenship

What are confederate organizations?

Any mining, trading, engineering, military or even minor political organization can ask to apply to the D.C.P (Delta Confederate Plan). Thus making it a confederate organization of the Delta. This pact adds rights and duties to the concerned actors :

  • More rights in the City, mainly focused on transports
  • Same military protection than the rest of the Delta Confederation
  • Having the possibility to vote for the Delta Directives
  • Having an easier access to the universal trade
  • Having a financial support in any project that the Delta finds attractive
  • Have to pay a variable tax depending on the situation
  • For any confederate organization with an armed force : to protect the other allied and confederate organizations
  • Obey the Delta Laws and directives
  • Be active in game and in the Delta sphere

Are confederate organizations independent?

If the confederate organizations respects the D.C.P duties, they have full freedom on their own laws. Apart from what the Delta already dictates.

Has an organization to become confederate to be allied?

No. Allies are not confederate organizations. You can find the list of our allies and confederate organizations in the "Partners" tab on top of this page.

How can my organization become an ally of the Delta Confederation?

For any alliance proposal, please join our Discord and ask to a legate.

How can I contact the Delta Confederation?

The easiest way to contact us is by joining our Discord Server. The link to access it and other contact methods are available in the "Contact" tab on top of this page.
